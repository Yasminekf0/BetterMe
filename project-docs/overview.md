# Master Trainer — AI That Coaches Like a Human

## 项目概述 / Project Overview

### 中文版

**项目名称**: AliCloud Sales Readiness Lab (阿里云销售就绪实验室)

**项目愿景**: 构建一个AI驱动的培训和辅导系统，通过自然对话的方式训练销售人员，实现规模化、个性化的销售技能培训。

**核心问题**:
- 新产品、新定价、新合规要求不断涌现
- 认证考试测试知识，但不测试执行能力
- 销售代表在实际异议处理、定位和技术清晰度方面存在困难
- 经理缺乏时间进行规模化辅导
- 培训内容很快过时

**解决方案**:
Master Trainer是一个AI教练，能够：
- 模拟真实的企业买家对话
- 评估回答并提供可解释的、基于证据的反馈
- 生成基于对话上下文的跟进邮件
- 允许培训团队即时更新场景

**目标用户**:
1. 销售代表/解决方案架构师
2. 培训/销售运营团队
3. 管理者（未来阶段）

---

### English Version

**Project Name**: AliCloud Sales Readiness Lab

**Vision**: Build an AI-powered training and coaching system that trains salespeople through natural conversation, achieving scalable and personalized sales skill training.

**Core Problems**:
- Constant new products, pricing, and compliance requirements
- Certifications test knowledge, not execution ability
- Sales reps struggle with live objections, positioning, and technical clarity
- Managers lack time to coach at scale
- Training content becomes outdated quickly

**Solution**:
Master Trainer is an AI coach that can:
- Simulate realistic enterprise buyer conversations
- Evaluate responses and provide explainable, evidence-based feedback
- Generate conversation-aware follow-up emails
- Allow enablement teams to update scenarios instantly

**Target Users**:
1. Sales Representatives / Solution Architects
2. Enablement / Sales Operations Teams
3. Managers (Future Phase)
